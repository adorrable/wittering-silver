Frequently Asked Questions

What safety measures are in place?

We follow strict hygiene protocols, sanitize equipment between guests, and ensure comfortable spacing in every seating area.

What should I expect on my first visit?

We start with a consultation, then tailor the look to your hair type and lifestyle, followed by precision cutting and finishing touches.

Do you offer services for all genders?

Yes. Our team serves diverse clients with a range of grooming needs and styles.

How long does a typical appointment take?

A standard cut takes about 45 minutes; a full grooming session may be longer depending on requests.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Walk-ins are welcome, subject to availability. We recommend booking to secure your preferred time.

What products do you use?

We use high-quality, professional grooming products designed for lasting results and comfort.
